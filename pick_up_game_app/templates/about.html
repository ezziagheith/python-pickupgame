About Hoopers

Ball For All

Hoopers was founded in 2019 with one objective: to bring people
together through the game of basketball without the pressure
of gym memberships or league play. We wanted to offer games when
and where works best for you.

Statement from Developers:

Our motivation began when we were hanging out one day, and wanted
to play a game of basketball. We couldn't find any gyms nearby or any pick-up games so we
decided to make our dream come true.
